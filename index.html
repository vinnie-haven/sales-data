AI Recap Coverage Gap

hs_call_summary vs hs_call_body — What We Learned

The AI recap that renders in HubSpot's UI (the formatted Summary / Key Notes / Topics Discussed block) is stored in hs_call_summary, not hs_call_body. Previous analysis was only querying hs_call_body, which explains why recaps appeared missing when they were actually present.

Coverage this week: ~37 of 61 substantive calls have hs_call_summary populated. The gap is entirely on Darren and Shelby's rep lines, and some of Michael's and Vilma's calls.

  • Jonathan and Ezra: strong AI recap coverage (~90%)
  • Vilma: partial — only SDR-handled calls have summaries
  • Michael: mixed — some calls have it, others don't
  • Darren and Shelby: zero hs_call_summary records this week

Root cause hypothesis: Dialpad AI transcription is controlled per-user or per-department. Darren and Shelby (and Vilma's rep line) likely have AI transcription off in Dialpad's AI Settings panel. Action: Dialpad admin → AI Settings → check per-user toggle for these reps.
